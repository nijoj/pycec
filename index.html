What is PyCEC ?

t’s the first effort by College of Engineering to host Python State level Students Conference under the Forum Of Computer Science Students (FOCES).Unlike any other Pycon mini conferences PyCec 2017, gives students a new opportunity to enlighten the knowledge about Python programming language and its wide possibilities. But honestly, it’s the people that make hacking in/on/with Python so fun and rewarding. There is a stereotype of programmers as anti-social, maladjusted, unpleasant people, but that stereotype just vanishes at PyCec. Whether chatting with total strangers who we will probably never meet again, or reconnecting with former colleagues, or meeting people who we have only “known” online before, PyCec is just about the friendliest and most welcoming environment . For every student, this will be a new experience as this is one of its kind.
